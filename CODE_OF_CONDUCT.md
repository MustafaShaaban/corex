# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

The Corex project is committed to providing a welcoming, harassment-free experience for everyone who
takes part — contributors, maintainers, and users alike — regardless of background or identity. We want
participation in the project to be a respectful and constructive experience.

## Our standards

We expect everyone in the community to:

- be considerate, respectful, and collaborative;
- welcome newcomers and assume good intent;
- give and accept constructive feedback gracefully;
- focus on what is best for the project and the wider community.

Behaviour that harasses, demeans, or excludes others is not acceptable. Maintainers may remove,
edit, or reject contributions, comments, and other interactions that are not aligned with this Code
of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently ban any participant for behaviour they judge harmful.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests, and
discussions — and whenever an individual is representing the project in public.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please report it privately to
**mustafashaaban22@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and
the reporter's confidentiality will be respected.
